How to Play

  • Step 1: Draw anything on the whiteboard — a human, cat, car, flower, cloud, or any creature you imagine!
  • Step 2: Click "Drop to Sandbox". The AI scanner will analyse your drawing's shape and guess what it is.
  • Step 3: Confirm the AI's guess with Yes or correct it with No and pick the right label.
  • Step 4: Choose an activity from the recommended list — each class has unique animations. The AI also tries to detect body parts (legs, mouth, etc.) for smarter movement!
  • Step 5: Need help with drawing? Ask Scribbie, our drawing assistant chatbot, by clicking the bottom-right icon in the whiteboard and entering a prompt like "how to draw a cat".
  • Interact: Drag and throw sketches around the sandbox. They react to gravity, friction, and wall collisions.
